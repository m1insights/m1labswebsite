About M1 Labs

We're on a mission to empower individuals to reach their full potential.

At M1 Labs, we believe everyone has untapped potential waiting to be unlocked. Our journey began with a simple question: how can technology help people become better versions of themselves?

This question led us to create two revolutionary apps that approach human potential from different angles:

  • Synq - For those who want to optimize their biology and understand what truly works for their unique physiology.
  • Blyss - For those seeking to enhance their mental state and performance through AI-powered meditation and personalized wellness experiences.

Our philosophy is rooted in the belief that small, consistent improvements lead to extraordinary results. Whether it's understanding how a supplement affects your focus or preparing mentally for an important presentation, our apps provide the tools to enhance your capabilities.

We combine cutting-edge technology with research-backed approaches to create experiences that are not just useful, but transformative. Our team is dedicated to continuous innovation and refinement, always seeking new ways to help our users achieve more in their daily lives.

M1 Labs isn't just about apps – it's about empowering a community of individuals committed to growth, self-improvement, and reaching new heights of human potential.
