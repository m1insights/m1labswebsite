About M1 Labs

We're on a mission to empower individuals to reach their full potential.

At M1 Labs, we harness the transformative power of artificial intelligence to help people achieve more, learn more, and become better versions of themselves. Our journey began with a conviction: AI should amplify human potential, not replace it.

We've created a suite of AI-powered applications that intelligently adapt to each user's unique needs:

  • Synq - AI-driven biohacking with multi-model intelligence (GPT-4, Claude, Gemini) that analyzes your biology and delivers evidence-based optimization strategies backed by 5,000+ scientific citations.
  • Blyss - AI-powered meditation and wellness companion that creates personalized mindfulness experiences using advanced voice synthesis and intelligent scheduling insights.

Our philosophy centers on intelligent personalization: AI should understand your unique context, goals, and preferences to deliver insights that are genuinely transformative. Whether it's AI analyzing how a specific supplement affects your cognitive performance or generating a custom meditation session tailored to your current stress level, our technology learns and adapts.

We combine the latest advances in artificial intelligence with rigorous scientific research to create experiences that are precise, personalized, and powerful. Our multi-AI architectures ensure reliability while our evidence-based approach guarantees that every recommendation is backed by real research, not generic advice.

M1 Labs is pioneering the future where AI becomes your personal optimization partner – helping you learn faster, perform better, and continuously evolve toward your highest potential.
